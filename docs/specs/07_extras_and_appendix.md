# 9. Extras English Vocabulary

- **Vocabulary Distinctions (GET vs TAKE):**
  - Use `GET` (got) for passive situations where two people are involved (someone gives you something). Use for transmitted conditions (illnesses) and legitimate obtaining (with permission).
  - Use `TAKE` (took) for active actions by one person. When used without permission, implies stealing. Use for self-administered actions (like pills).
  - **Transport:** Use `get to` (arrive at), `take to` (offer transport), `get in/out of` (car), `get on/off` (bus/train).
